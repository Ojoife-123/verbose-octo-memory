Five Famous Monuments In Africa, One From Each Region

  • North Africa: The Great Pyramid of Giza, Egypt
  • West Africa: The Great Mosque of Djenné, Mali
  • East Africa: The Rock-Hewn Churches of Lalibela, Ethiopia
  • Central Africa: The Amani Nature Reserve, Tanzania
  • Southern Africa: Table Mountain, South Africa

These monuments represent the rich cultural heritage and historical significance of their respective regions in Africa.
